Morgan Freeman (Actor) - On This Day

Profession: Actor

Biography: Known for his distinctively smooth, deep voice, he has received Academy Award nominations for his performances in "Street Smart", "Driving Miss Daisy", "The Shawshank Redemption" and "Invictus", and won the Best Supporting Actor Oscar in 2005 for Million Dollar Baby.

Born: June 1, 1937
Birthplace: Memphis, Tennessee, USA
Age: 87 years old

Generation: Silent Generation
Chinese Zodiac: Ox
Star Sign: Gemini
