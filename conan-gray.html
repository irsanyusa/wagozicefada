Conan Gray (Singer-Songwriter) - On This Day

Full Name: Conan Lee Gray
Profession: Singer-Songwriter

Biography: Conan Gray is an American singer, songwriter, and YouTuber. He gained fame in 2017 after releasing his debut single "Idle Town". Gray is known for his unique blend of alternative and pop music, which has earned him millions of devoted fans around the world.

Gray began writing and recording music at a young age. He attained internet fame in 2015 after posting his song "Generation Why" to YouTube, which has since been viewed over 13 million times.

Since then, Gray has released two EPs and his debut album "Kid Krow", which reached number 5 on the U.S. Billboard 200 chart. His single "Maniac" also charted on the Billboard Hot 100. Gray has also become known for his work on social media, where he has amassed over 6 million followers.
