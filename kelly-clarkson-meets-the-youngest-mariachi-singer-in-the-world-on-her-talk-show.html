Kelly Clarkson Meets The Youngest Mariachi Singer In The World On Her Talk Show

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Kelly Clarkson recently introduced the world to Mateo López, a young mariachi singer from San Antonio, Texas. López, who first gained international recognition as the world’s youngest mariachi singer at the age of four, showcased his incredible talent on the stage of The Kelly Clarkson Show on Wednesday, October 18.

Kelly Clarkson Invited The Youngest Mariachi Singer On Her Talk Show

The journey that brought López to Clarkson’s show began when the young musician’s management team reached out to the famous Texan singer. For those who may not know, Clarkson expressed her love of Spanish music during her time on NBC’s The Voice. The producers of The Kelly Clarkson Show were eager to have López on their program, but they had to wait for the end of the Hollywood writers’ strike before they could make it happen.

When the show resumed in October, they decided to give Adalberto López, López’s father, a call to arrange a Zoom call for a special segment on the show, called What I’m Liking? Clarkson explained that she wanted to celebrate Hispanic Heritage Month, even though it had already concluded by the time the new episodes were airing.

During their heartwarming interaction, Clarkson asked young López about his passion for mariachi music and where it all began. López shared that his journey into the world of mariachi started with his sister Ariella, who learned from his grandfather, Adalberto’s father. López’s talents quickly gained recognition, as he began performing in front of audiences around San Antonio.

He even appeared on the Mexican version of America’s Got Talent and NBC’s Little Big Shots. Remarkably, López still holds the Guinness World Record for being the youngest mariachi singer, earning this title when he was just four years old.

Clarkson’s emotional reaction to López’s performance on her show didn’t go unnoticed. Adalberto shared that the renowned singer was so touched that she extended an invitation to Mateo to appear on her show in person in the future.

Mateo López’s 2023 XFL Championship Performance Went Viral

López recently delivered a show-stopping performance at the 2023 XFL Championship Game. The stage was set, and López was given the honor of singing the national anthem, “The Star-Spangled Banner.” It was a moment that would go down in history as one of his most exceptional performances to date.

With everyone watching, López sang the anthem with lots of passion and talent. When he finished the song, the crowd clapped and cheered loudly, showing how much they loved his performance. Even the Arlington Renegades’ head coach, Bob Stoops, was really excited and cheered for López.

López’s amazing show at the 2023 XFL Championship suggests he has a great future in music. He has a special talent that appeals to people of all ages.
