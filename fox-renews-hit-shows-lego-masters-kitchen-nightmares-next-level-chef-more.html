Fox Renews Hit Shows 'LEGO Masters,' 'Kitchen Nightmares,' 'Next Level Chef', More

Fox has been making some noteworthy decisions regarding the fate of their scripted and unscripted television content. Recently, they made the exciting announcement that LEGO Masters would be returning for a fifth season. This announcement excited the show’s dedicated fans.

Fox Announces LEGO Masters Season 5 Renewal

The news of LEGO Masters Season 5’s renewal came in September 2023, just ahead of the Season 4 premiere. Hosted by the talented Will Arnett, this reality show has captured the hearts of viewers with its unique blend of creativity, humor, and, of course, building with everyone’s favorite childhood toy, Lego.

The show, which first premiered on February 5, 2020, has consistently delivered entertainment and joy to its audience. It features 12 teams of talented builders who compete against each other to create intricate Lego creations. The winners not only receive the title of “LEGO MASTERS” but also a substantial cash prize and the coveted “ultimate LEGO trophy.”

Season 4 of LEGO Masters is set to continue the winning formula that has made it a favorite among viewers. Will Arnett returns as the charismatic host, guiding viewers through the challenges and creations of the competing teams. Brickmasters Amy and Jamie, who hail from Lego headquarters in Denmark, will once again serve as judges, evaluating each team’s work based on creativity, storytelling, technical ability, and the element of surprise.

This season promises to be even more exciting and challenging, with new, unexpected twists that will test the builders’ skills and creativity. The competition will feature a diverse range of teams, including grandparents and their grandchildren, a mom-son duo, and many more, adding a unique flavor to the show.

Kitchen Nightmares, Next Level Chef Are Also Renewed For New Seasons

Fox’s recent decisions regarding television content go beyond just renewing LEGO Masters. In a surprising turn of events, the network has also decided to revive two well-loved culinary shows: Kitchen Nightmares and Next Level Chef among others.

Culinary enthusiasts will smile at the news that Kitchen Nightmares is making a comeback. This reality show, which famed chef Gordon Ramsay originally hosted, achieved massive success when it first aired. However, in 2014, Ramsay announced that he would no longer produce new episodes of the show.

Fast forward to 2023, and Kitchen Nightmares is getting a fresh lease on life. Fox has given the green light for an eighth season of the show, which premiered its first episode on September 25, 2023.

Another culinary gem that has secured its spot on Fox’s lineup is Next Level Chef. This reality competition show has been a hit since its inception, and Fox has rewarded its success with a two-season renewal. This means that fans can look forward to the culinary battles and innovative dishes for at least two more seasons.
