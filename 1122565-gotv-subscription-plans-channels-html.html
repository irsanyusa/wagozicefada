GOtv Nigeria guide: packages, channels list, prices, contacts

GOtv Nigeria is among the most preferred cable TV for millions of people residing in Nigeria. One of the reasons why GOtv is prevalent in this country is because of the availability of different packages at different prices. Also, bouquets have different channels that meet the needs of diverse viewers. Check out the current GOtv Nigeria packages, channels list, and costs below.

GOtv Nigeria belongs to Multichoice Group - which also owns SuperSport, M-Net, and DStv Media. There are slight differences between GOtv and DStv. For instance, the number of HD channels is different on both platforms. Also, the DStv media depends on satellite dishes while GOtv uses Digital terrestrial television (DTT) technology. Therefore, weather conditions can affect the quality of the signals on DStv, while the strength of GOtv signals is affected by terrain and weather patterns.

GOtv self-service in Nigeria

The GOtv self-service portal allows subscribers to access customer care services online. Registering on the portal is free. After that, you can log into the portal via the GOtv website or mobile app.

You only need your GOtv IUC number, IUC card number, and mobile number/surname to access most services. Most importantly, always ensure these details are updated with GOtv.

Your IUC number is on an orange sticker under your decoder, while the IUC card number is on your smartcard number. When logging into the portal on any GOtvs digital platform (website and app), you will need your IUC card number and mobile number/surname to make a payment, fix decoder errors, change package, and more.

How to clear error code on GOtv Nigeria?

You can clear error codes on the GOtv self-service portal, which is accessible via the GOtv website or mobile app. Alternatively, you can dial USSD code *288# and follow the prompts to clear the error appearing on your TV screen.

What is the GOtv customer care number in Nigeria?

If you cannot access the GOtv self-service portal or encounter challenges when using it, you can call the GOtv Nigeria customer care number at +234 803 904 4688 or send an email to GOtvNigeria@multichoice.co.za.

What is the GOtv customer care number in Nigeria?

GOtv customer care helpline Nigeria is +234 803 904 4688. You can also email them via GOtvNigeria@multichoice.co.za or chat with them on the GOtv website.
