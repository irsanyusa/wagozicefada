JoJo Siwa Making an Animated Series Based on XOMG POP!

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Alberto E. Rodriguez/Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

JoJo Siwa recently revealed a collaboration with Pure Imagination Productions to create an animated children’s series based on XOMG POP! Followers of Siwa and her girl group are buzzing over this news.

XOMG POP! to Be Featured in An Animated Series

Pure Imagination Studios is a renowned animation studio that has produced several successful shows, including Monster Hunter: Legends of the Guild and The Freak Brothers. The studio’s expertise in creating animated content, combined with Siwa’s creative vision, is expected to produce a show that will appeal to young audiences.

The series will follow the adventures of the six members of XOMG Pop! “as they tour the known and the unknown universe in a magical, mysterious tour bus.”

According to sources, the animated kids series based on XOMG POP! that Siwa is developing will be launched on TikTok once it is completed. This move is expected to appeal to the show’s target audience and also leverage TikTok’s massive user base.

The announcement of this collaboration comes at a time when the demand for animated content is at an all-time high. With streaming platforms such as Netflix and Disney+ investing heavily in original animated content, the future looks bright for the animation industry.

JoJo Siwa Praises XOMG POP! for Their Stellar Onstage Performances

Siwa recently took to Instagram to share her pride for the all-girl pop group XOMG Pop! The dancer and co-creator of the group, shared a series of photos from the show, which also showcased their impressive choreography and energy on stage.

The post shows the six members of XOMG POP! performing in colorful outfits and choreographed dance moves. Siwa then captioned the post with a simple heartfelt message, “Proud XOMGPOP.”

XOMG POP! have been making waves in the music industry with their catchy pop songs and also dynamic performances by the group. At this time, Siwa remains as a mentor and collaborator for the group. For those who may not know, the dancer also choreographs their performances.
