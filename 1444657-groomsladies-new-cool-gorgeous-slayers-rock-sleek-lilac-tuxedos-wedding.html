Groomsladies is the New Cool: Gorgeous Slayers Rock Sleek Lilac Tuxedos for Wedding

Over the weekend, a group of ladies set the internet ablaze when photos of them on 'groomswomen' duties surfaced on social media.

If you think only men know how to pull off sleek looks in tuxedos then you may want to have a rethink as some ladies rocked the look effortlessly at a recent wedding.

While the role of groomsmen was originally for men alone - as the title explains, that rule has since been considered antiquated as, over the years, more men have featured women on their wedding train.

In the photos which were shared via @sugarweddings, the ladies are seen sporting lilac 3-piece suits with black bowties and sleek boutonnieres.

Each groomswoman rocked a pair of black heels, creating a uniform style.
