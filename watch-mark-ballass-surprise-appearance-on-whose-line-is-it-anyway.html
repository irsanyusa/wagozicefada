Watch Mark Ballas's Surprise Appearance on 'Whose Line Is It Anyway?'

Mark Ballas may be out of regular television at the moment, but that hasn’t stopped him from occasionally showing up on screen. The retired Dancing With The Stars pro recently surprised his fans with a fun appearance on game show Whose Line Is It Anyway?

Mark Ballas Makes Guest Appearance on Whose Line Is It Anyway?

During a recent episode of the game show, Ballas showed a quirkier version of himself. The dancing pro guested to perform a theater-style musical number with host Wayne Brady. During the appearance, the two roasted each other’s mothers. Later, Ballas came back for the skit “Living Scenery.”

On Instagram, Ballas teased a clip of his appearance on the show, which got his fans and friends hysterical. He shared a snippet from the episode where he portrayed dolphins alongside Brady. The two were also seen copying funny “dolphin noises.”

Upon seeing the clip, several people then expressed their happiness over the DWTS pro’s guest appearance.

“Shut up! You didn’t tell me you were going to be on this show!” one person wrote.

“The man can do it all! Legend!” another person wrote.

“He was so good! Probably one of their best guests in recent years!” a fan wrote on Reddit.

After securing a Mirrorball trophy with TikTok sensation Charli D’Amelio on DWTS season 31, Ballas announced the he’s already retiring as a pro dancer on the show. While fans don’t see Ballas on television as often as before, he doesn’t totally reject appearance gigs. He was present during DWTS’s tribute to late head judge Len Goodman. Furthermore, the choreographer appeared son DWTS season 32 finale.

He Announced Birth of His First Child

Last year, Ballas shared a family milestone to his fans on social media. The DWTS revealed that he and partner Britney Jean Carlson have welcomed their first son, Banksi Wylde Ballas, in November 2023. The couple announced the good news on Instagram, posting a black-and-white picture of their hands holding their son.

“Welcome to Earth my son,” Ballas wrote.

“Happy one month sweet boy. You are so loved,” Jean wrote.

Back in June, the couple revealed that their family was growing after they posted a clip on social media saying they created a “tiny human.”

The couple welcomed Banksi after suffering a miscarriage in 2022. The pair shared the devastating experience on their YouTube channel months ago. Ballas and Jean, who perform as Alexander Jean, reflected on the loss through their single “Rainbow.”

“We lost a baby last year,” they wrote. “Just when we thought we were in a good place to tell friends + family we received the heavy news that our little one wasn’t going to make it, we’ve both been through hard things in our lives but this was a different kind of sadness.”
