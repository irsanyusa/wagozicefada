Famous Audrey Hepburn quotes from the movies

The actress passed away in 1993 but many movie and fashion lovers will attest that Audrey Hepburn quotes are still relatable and inspiring. Her style has inspired and influenced many beautiful red carpet looks today, and her words of wisdom motivate people in life.

Audrey was more than just a pretty face. Born as Audrey Kathleen Ruston in Belgium on 4th May 1929, she starred in the Broadway production of Gigi. Ten years later she went on to play her biggest role to date as Holly Golightly in the movie Breakfast at Tiffany's. Quotes by Audrey Hepburn are derived from her movie roles, interviews and life experiences.
