The Gate Pa - On This Day

published on August 21, 2024
The Gate Pa was designed by Ngai Te Rangi leader Pene Taka and was perhaps the most remarkable Maori pa ever constructed. Situated on the border of missionary and Maori land the pa stood on what is now the modern day city of Tauranga.
