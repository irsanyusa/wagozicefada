The Last Of Us (TV Series)

published on July 25, 2024
"The Last of Us" is a 2023 American drama series from HBO adapted from the 2013 Naughty Dog video game of the same name. Created by Craig Mazin and Neil Druckmann, it explores a post-apocalyptic world 20 years after the onset of a pandemic. Pedro Pascal stars as Joel, a smuggler asked to transport a teenage girl named Ellie (Bella Ramsey) across the country, as she is immune to the fungal infection that has ravaged society and turned many into zombie-like creatures.
