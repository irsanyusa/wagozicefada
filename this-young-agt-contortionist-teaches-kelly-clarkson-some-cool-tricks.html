Season 15 AGT contestant, Emerald Gordon Wulf appeared on The Kelly Clarkson Show this week, showing off her incredible contortionist skills. The 14-year-old spoke to Clarkson about the realization of her talent and what led up to her eventual television success.

‘AGT’ Contortionist Wows Kelly Clarkson With Incredible Tricks

Former America’s Got Talent contestant, Emerald Gordon Wulf recently met Kelly Clarkson on NBC’s The Kelly Clarkson Show, showing off her incredible contortionist skills after appearing on AGT early last year.

The 14-year-old entered the studio, crawling in an unimaginable body position, followed by gasps from the audience. Clarkson from first sight seemed shocked at the sight of the contortionist’s skills. Speaking on first discovering her talent, Wulf mentioned that she used to do gymnastics at the age of six, but discovered she was a ‘failure at it’.

She added: “But then, I saw a circus show and I took some pictures, and I was like ‘That looks really fun’ so when I got home I tried to do some of the poses and I actually got really close.”

Emerald told Kelly she spends ‘three or four hours a day’ practicing her skills before attempting to teach Clarkson a challenging yoga stretch in front of the live audience. “It’s very common in yoga, it’s called the seal pose,” Wulf told Clarkson. The two proceeded to lay on the studio carpet, sitting into a full body stretch with a straight back.

After the interview, Emerald performed a socially-distanced long-form routine for the studio audience and those watching at home.

Emerald Gordon Wulf’s Life After ‘America’s Got Talent’

Due to COVID-19 restrictions, Emerald Gordon Wulf has had to practice her contortionist routines at home. However, Wulf has kept her Instagram and TikTok followers updated on all her latest skills and stretches.

Wulf has received thousands of likes on her at-home videos, with a recent video showing Emerald balancing on canes as she performed her extraordinary body twisting routine. Another popular video on the TikTok platform, shows the young star performing her talent in a supermarket with a shopping trolley before doing the splits whilst grocery shopping.
