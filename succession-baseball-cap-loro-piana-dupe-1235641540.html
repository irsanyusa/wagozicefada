Just in Time for Gift-Giving Season, Get This Succession-Style Baseball Cap (and Loro Pi

Loro Piana's cashmere cap retails for over $500, but we found a U.S.-made brand selling similar hats for less than one-tenth the price

“Succession” may be over, but the show’s impact is living on, with the HBO series landing a nine nominations for the 2024 Golden Globe Awards. “Succession’s” sartorial influence continues to grow too, with the characters’ outfits and accessories spawning almost as many “get the look” articles as the series did think pieces. One of the most searched-for clothing items on “Succession was also one of the most simple: Kendall Roy’s baseball cap.

In addition to his custom suits, Jacques Marie Mage sunglasses and Gucci sneakers, Roy’s outfits were often finished off with a fitted, logo-less baseball cap from Italian brand Loro Piana. Roy’s portrayer Jeremy Strong was such a fan of the hats that he admitted to wearing the cap even when he wasn’t filming the show.

But this isn’t just any regular baseball cap — Loro Piana makes its hat from high-end Italian materials like cashmere, and the resulting price point is a similarly high-end $500+ online.

Fortunately, we’ve found a great Loro Piana dupe at Love Your Melon — a U.S. accessories brand that donates 50% of profits to organizations fighting pediatric cancer. Love Your Melon sells a $40 sueded baseball cap online that’s a popular Loro Piana alternative.

BUY: LOVE YOUR MELON SUEDE CAP $40

Made in the U.S., the black hat is a classic five-panel cap made from a super smooth sueded material. It features a black leather melon patch on the back of the cap and is finished with an adjustable strap.

Prefer a cashmere version? Lora Piana is known for its neutral, earth-tone cashmere hats, which are also in the $500 range. Love Your Melon, meantime, sells a beige cashmere cap for $92 — just one-fifth the price.
