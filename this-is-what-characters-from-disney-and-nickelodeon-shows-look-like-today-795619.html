This Is What Characters From Disney and Nickelodeon Shows Look Like Today

In the past, Disney and Nickelodeon knew how to produce series that made us laugh out loud. Since the year 2000, we enjoyed lots of kids’ shows (and some not so childish) on television, which entertained us in our spare time. They also introduced us to many actors who stole our hearts, and who, after becoming famous, continued on with their lives on and off the screen. Some even became superstars, like Miley Cyrus and Selena Gomez, who have millions of followers on social media today.

Bright Side collected some current photos for you of the most memorable characters from popular shows like Hannah Montana, Zoey 101, and Lizzie McGuire.
