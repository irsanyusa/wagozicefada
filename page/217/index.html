If you've never heard of nkwobi, kindly allow us explain. Nkwobi is an Eastern Nigerian dish consisting of cow leg, potash puree, palm oil and spices. To achieve this tasty treat, the cowleg is boiled until it is very tender. Nkwobi is also usually served in a special bowl that looks like a mini mortar.
